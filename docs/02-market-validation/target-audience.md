# BizVibe - Target Audience

## Overview

BizVibe serves a two-sided marketplace: consumers discovering businesses and businesses seeking customers. This document defines our target audience segments in detail.

## Audience Insights

### Consumer Insights

**Why They'll Use BizVibe**:
1. **Discovery is fun**: Scrolling is entertaining, not a chore
2. **Authenticity matters**: Video shows the real business
3. **Local is valuable**: Supporting community feels good
4. **Convenience wins**: From discovery to purchase, all in-app
5. **Social validation**: See what others are loving

**What Will Make Them Stay**:
- Consistently relevant content
- New discoveries daily
- Easy transactions
- Good customer experiences
- Community feeling

### Business Insights

**Why They'll Use BizVibe**:
1. **Easy video creation**: No expertise needed
2. **Guaranteed local reach**: Not competing globally
3. **Clear ROI**: See views, messages, sales
4. **Direct commerce**: No website needed
5. **Level playing field**: Compete with big brands

**What Will Make Them Stay**:
- Actual customers from platform
- Measurable ROI
- Easy content creation
- Responsive customer support
- Growing platform reach
