# BizVibe - User Personas

## Overview

This document provides detailed user personas for both consumer and business users. These personas guide product decisions, feature prioritization, and UX design.

## Persona Matrix

### Consumer Personas

| Attribute | Sarah (Explorer) | Marcus (Hunter) | Jennifer (Loyalist) |
|-----------|------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **Age** | 28 | 24 | 38 |
| **Primary Goal** | Discovery | Deals | Community |
| **Usage Pattern** | Daily browse | Alert-driven | Weekly deep dive |
| **Purchase Style** | Impulse | Deal-driven | Relationship |
| **Retention Risk** | Low | Medium | Very Low |
| **Revenue Potential** | High | Medium | High |

| Attribute | Maria (Restaurant) | Alex (Boutique) |
|-----------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Business Size** | 15 employees | 3 employees |
| **Tech Comfort** | Moderate | High |

| **Primary Goal** | Fill seats | Sell inventory |
| **Content Style** | Simple, authentic | Polished, trendy |
| **Success Metric** | Reservations | Sales |

## Design Implications

### For Sarah (Explorer)
- Endless, engaging feed
- Easy saving and sharing
- Business story content

### For Marcus (Hunter)
- Strong notification system
- Quick purchase flow
- Deal highlighting

### For Jennifer (Loyalist)
- Business backgrounds visible
- Review system important
- Repeat purchase features

### For Maria (Restaurant)
- Ultra-simple video upload
- Clear analytics
- Minimal time investment

### For Alex (Boutique)
- Product tagging
- Inventory management
- Commerce features
