# BizVibe - Revenue Strategy

## Overview

BizVibe employs a multi-stream revenue model that balances:
- **Accessibility**: Low barrier to entry for businesses
- **Scalability**: Revenue grows with platform usage
- **Sustainability**: Diversified income prevents single-point dependency

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Business Subscriptions (Primary) - Target: 40% of Revenue

Recurring monthly/annual subscriptions for business accounts.

| Tier | Monthly | Annual | Target % of Businesses |
|------|---------|--------|----------------------|
| Free | $0 | $0 | 60% |
| Starter | $19 | $190 | 25% |
| Pro | $49 | $490 | 12% |
| Enterprise | $149 | $1,490 | 3% |

**Projected Revenue (at 10,000 businesses):**
- 2,500 Starter × $19 = $47,500/mo
- 1,200 Pro × $49 = $58,800/mo
- 300 Enterprise × $149 = $44,700/mo
- **Total: $151,000/month**

---

### 2. Video Boost & Promotion (Secondary) - Target: 25% of Revenue

Pay-per-promotion model for increased video visibility.

| Product | Price | Margin |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Video Boosts | $5-$50 | 70% |
| Featured Placement | $25/week | 80% |
| Category Sponsorship | $100/week | 85% |
| Homepage Feature | $500/day | 90% |

**Key Metrics:**
- Target: 10% of businesses boost monthly
- Average boost spend: $30/month
- At 10,000 businesses: $30,000/month

---

### 3. Transaction Fees - Target: 20% of Revenue

Commission on in-app purchases and bookings.

| Transaction Type | Fee |
|-----------------|-----|
| Product Purchase | 5% |
| Service Booking | 5% |
| Gift Card Purchase | 3% |
| Tip/Donation | 2% |

**Payment Processing:**
- Stripe fee: 2.9% + $0.30 (pass-through to business)
- BizVibe margin: Above rates (kept by platform)

**Projected at $500K GMV/month:**
- Average take rate: 4.5%
- Revenue: $22,500/month

---

### 4. Add-On Purchases - Target: 10% of Revenue

One-time and consumable purchases.

| Add-On | Price | Target Buyers |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Extra Video Pack (5) | $5 | 5% of Free users |
| Verified Badge | $50 | 10% of businesses |
| Premium Video Slot | $10 | 15% of paid users |
| Extended Retention | $2/video | 8% of users |

---

### 5. Consumer Premium (Future) - Target: 5% of Revenue

Subscription for power users.

**BizVibe+ ($4.99/month)**
- Ad-free experience
- Early access to deals
- Exclusive offers
- Priority customer support
- Enhanced wishlist features

Target: 2% of active consumers
At 100,000 users: 2,000 × $4.99 = $9,980/month

## Key Metrics to Track

### Subscription Metrics
- **MRR** (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- **ARR** (Annual Recurring Revenue)
- **ARPU** (Average Revenue Per User)
- **Churn Rate** (% cancellations)
- **LTV** (Lifetime Value)
- **Upgrade Rate** (Free → Paid)

### Transaction Metrics
- **GMV** (Gross Merchandise Value)
- **Take Rate** (% of GMV kept)
- **Transaction Volume**
- **Average Order Value**

### Promotion Metrics
- **Boost Adoption Rate**
- **Average Boost Spend**
- **Boost ROI** (for businesses)
- **Cost Per View** (for boosted content)

## Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring
- Enterprise tier anchors Pro as "reasonable"
- Annual pricing shows monthly savings

### Loss Aversion
- Video retention limits create urgency
- "Videos will archive" messaging

### Social Proof
- Badge systems (Pro, Featured)
- Success stories from upgraded businesses

### Gradual Value Reveal
- Free tier shows analytics teaser
- "Upgrade to see demographics"

## Risk Mitigation

### Revenue Concentration Risk
- No single revenue stream > 40%
- Diversified across subscriptions, transactions, promotions

### Churn Risk
- Annual discount incentivizes commitment
- Strong onboarding and success tracking
- Proactive churn prevention (usage alerts)

### Pricing Power Risk
- Clear value differentiation between tiers
- Regular feature additions to paid tiers
- Competitor monitoring
