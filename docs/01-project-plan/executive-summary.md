# BizVibe - Executive Summary

## Project Overview

**BizVibe** is a mobile-first social commerce platform that transforms how consumers discover local businesses and how businesses market themselves. By combining TikTok-style short-form video with seamless e-commerce, BizVibe creates an engaging marketplace where businesses pitch their offerings through 15-60 second videos, and users scroll to discover, connect, and purchase.

---

## Vision

**To become the go-to platform for local commerce discovery**, making it as easy and engaging to find great local businesses as it is to scroll through social media.

## Mission

Empower local businesses with accessible, effective video marketing tools while giving consumers an enjoyable way to discover and support their community.

---

## The Opportunity

### Market Gap
- **$1.2 trillion** social commerce market by 2025
- TikTok proved video-driven commerce works, but focuses on global brands
- No dedicated platform for **local, video-native commerce**
- Small businesses struggle with video marketing complexity
- Consumers want authentic, engaging business discovery

### Target Market
- **Primary Markets**: Urban/suburban areas in the US
- **Initial Focus**: Single metro area for MVP validation
- **Expansion**: 5 cities by Year 1, nationwide by Year 2

## Technology Approach

### Stack Overview

| Layer | Technology | Rationale |
|-------|------------|-----------|
| Mobile | Expo + React Native | Cross-platform, fast development |
| Backend | Supabase | Rapid development, built-in features |
| Video | Mux | Scalable, reliable CDN |
| Payments | Stripe Connect | Industry standard, marketplace support |
| Web (Phase 2) | Next.js + Vercel | React ecosystem |

### Key Technical Advantages
- **70% faster development** with Supabase vs custom backend
- **Single codebase** for iOS and Android
- **OTA updates** without app store review
- **Enterprise-grade video** infrastructure via Mux
- **Compliant payments** via Stripe

## Competitive Advantage

### Why BizVibe Wins

1. **Local Focus**: Unlike TikTok Shop (global), we prioritize local discovery
2. **Business-First**: Purpose-built for business promotion, not adapted
3. **Simplified Creation**: Anyone can make a video, no expertise needed
4. **Direct Commerce**: From discovery to purchase in one app
5. **Community Commerce**: Users become advocates, not just buyers

### Competitive Moat
- Network effects: More businesses → more content → more users → more businesses
- Local data advantage: Understanding of local markets
- Business relationships: Direct partnerships with local businesses
- Algorithm tuning: Optimized for commerce conversion
